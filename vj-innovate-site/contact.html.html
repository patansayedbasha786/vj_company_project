Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

What services do you offer?

We offer comprehensive software development services including e-commerce development, web applications, mobile apps, and custom software solutions. We also provide programming courses and internship programs.

How long are your courses?

Our courses range from 2 months to 6 months depending on the complexity and depth of the subject. We offer both part-time and full-time options to accommodate different schedules.

Do you provide job placement assistance?

Yes, we provide 100% placement assistance for our internship program graduates. We have partnerships with various companies and help with resume building, interview preparation, and job connections.

Can I take courses online?

We offer both in-person and online course options. Our online courses include live sessions, recorded lectures, and hands-on projects with mentor support.

What are the prerequisites for your courses?

Prerequisites vary by course. Basic computer literacy is required for all courses. Some advanced courses may require prior programming experience. Check individual course descriptions for specific requirements.
